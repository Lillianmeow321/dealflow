Subject: VoltBridge Mobility - export growth quality review

Team,

We are looking at VoltBridge Mobility, a China-based e-bike company exporting to Europe and Southeast Asia. The company is seeking growth capital to expand overseas channels, add working capital, and build localized service capability.

Management's headline story is attractive: revenue is up strongly, Europe remains the largest market, and Southeast Asia is scaling quickly. They are positioning the company as a category winner with Chinese supply-chain advantage and a growing overseas brand.

Before we move further, I want a PE-style operating review. Please do not just summarize revenue growth. I want to know whether the growth is high quality.

Specific questions:

- Is revenue growth coming from healthy channels or lower-margin / higher-risk channels?
- How concentrated is the European distributor base?
- Are inventory days and aged inventory worsening?
- Are warranty and return costs rising, especially around battery and motor issues?
- Are regulatory, certification, battery transport, or tariff costs fully reflected in the plan?
- Should we proceed, request a revised operating plan, or pause?

Please use only the data room materials and prepare a KPI check, IC-style note, risk log, and management follow-up email.
