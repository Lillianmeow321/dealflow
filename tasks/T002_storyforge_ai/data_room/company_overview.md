# StoryForge AI Company Overview

## Company

StoryForge AI is an AI-native content studio producing short-form comic dramas, vertical manga series, and serialized visual fiction for mobile-first audiences. The company distributes content through TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Reels, Webtoon-style platforms, and its own StoryForge app.

## Product and Workflow

StoryForge uses a semi-automated production pipeline:

- Human writers create the core story arc, character bible, and episode outline.
- Generative AI tools create character concepts, backgrounds, panels, and rough motion.
- Human editors refine dialogue, pacing, continuity, and moderation.
- The distribution team tests hooks and episode variants across short-video platforms.
- Successful series are pushed into the StoryForge app, where users can pay to unlock later episodes or bonus endings.

## Management's Key Claims

- AI reduced average cost per episode by 64%.
- AI reduced average production cycle time from 14 days to 4.5 days.
- Monthly episode output increased from 18 to 74 episodes.
- Top series have reached more than 40M aggregate views.
- Management reports a 49% episode completion rate across launched series.
- Management reports 11% paid conversion among "engaged viewers."
- The company believes its advantage is not any single model, but a fast testing loop, genre-specific prompts, reusable character systems, and audience feedback data.

## Business Model

StoryForge monetizes through:

- In-app paid episode unlocks.
- Platform creator revenue share.
- Sponsorship placements in top-performing series.
- Future IP licensing and adaptation rights.

The company has not yet signed meaningful IP licensing deals. Most revenue currently comes from in-app unlocks and platform revenue share.

## Fundraise

StoryForge is raising $9M to:

- Build a larger production slate.
- Hire story editors and IP/legal review staff.
- Improve its proprietary story testing dashboard.
- Acquire paid traffic for the app.
- Develop a small number of premium original IP franchises.

## Management's Recommendation

Management argues that AI-native production turns content from a hit-driven craft business into a faster, data-driven portfolio model. They recommend investors focus on the cost reduction, output velocity, and the early performance of their top three series.
