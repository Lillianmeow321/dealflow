Before we take another meeting, I want a grounded view on whether this is real household demand or mostly demo excitement. Please review the data room and prepare:

1. A KPI sanity check, especially around usage retention, hardware margin, manufacturing yield, and warranty assumptions.
2. A short investment memo with a clear Proceed / Hold / Reject recommendation.
3. A risk log and the top follow-up questions for the founder.

Specific questions I care about:

- Are people still using the robot after the novelty wears off?
- Which use case looks most real: elderly care, child interaction, or general family assistant?
- Does the hardware margin work after warranty/return costs?
- Is the manufacturing plan credible given the pilot yield?
- Is there defensible AI/product differentiation, or are they wrapping third-party models in hardware?
